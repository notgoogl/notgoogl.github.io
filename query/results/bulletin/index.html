MISSING PERSONS BULLETIN — Federal Bureau of Investigation

Jordan Whitaker

Age: 34 | Last Seen: August 3, 2023 — Hollowridge Township

Summary:

Whitaker, a part-time mechanic and musician, was last seen leaving her workshop late at night. CCTV footage shows her pausing at the end of the street, staring upward for approximately two minutes before walking out of frame. Her phone was recovered in an empty parking lot the next morning, displaying an unsent message draft that read only, "I think it's calling again."

Relevant Notes:

  • Lost her partner in an apartment fire one year prior.
  • Generally social at work, only occasionally works late alone.
  • No reported compulsions or recurring dreams in recent months.

Status: Missing — suspicious circumstances.

Investigators could not verify the subject's presence at the scene when they arrived.

Relevant Notes:

  • Minor academic setback; recently expelled from university.
  • Travels occasionally with friends; not consistently alone.
  • No significant personal tragedy reported.
